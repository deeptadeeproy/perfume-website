THE MAGIC OF A UNIQUE OLFACTORY PORTRAIT

Each Bespoke Fragrance is born of an unforgettable encounter between our Master Perfumer and the client. A dialogue is established to define a unique olfactory universe that reflects their personality: tastes, passions, first memories… Our Master Perfumer devotes themselves entirely to crafting the formula. Very often the creative exchange resumes, as many times as necessary. Being the exclusive owner of their formula, the client can reorder their perfume throughout their lifetime.
